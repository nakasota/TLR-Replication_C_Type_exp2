==== [Issue Title] ====
proposal: crypto/elliptic: Add Sub() method to Curve interface

==== [Issue Body] ====
Elliptic crypto operations allow compute sum of two points.

There is also another operation needed for some algorithms - subtract one EC point from another. It would be great to have `Sub()` (or maybe call it `Minus()`) operation as a part of `elliptic.Curve` interface.
