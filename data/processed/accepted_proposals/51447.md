==== [Issue Title] ====
x/exp/winfsnotify: delete package

==== [Issue Body] ====
The `x/exp/winfsnotify` package was moved from the standard library to the `x/exp` repository in [CL 7456047](https://golang.org/cl/7456047), a little over nine years ago. It has apparently not been touched since then except to add an `// import` comment in 2014. In 2016, users discussed switching to an externally-maintained alternative (see @nathany's comment in https://github.com/golang/go/issues/17244#issuecomment-250935437).

At least one such alternative, [`github.com/fsnotify/fsnotify`](https://pkg.go.dev/github.com/fsnotify/fsnotify), was still actively maintained at least as of this January (see https://github.com/fsnotify/fsnotify/issues/413), and has a number of volunteers to maintain it going forward.

In the meantime `x/exp/winfsnotify` has rotted. It has no active owners and its only substantial test is flaky (#49734). The corresponding `fsnotify` package for Unix platforms was removed in [CL 3990](https://golang.org/cl/3990), back in 2015.

The packages in the `x/exp` repository are explicitly experimental. I propose that we declare the `x/exp/winfsnotify` experiment concluded and delete the package.
