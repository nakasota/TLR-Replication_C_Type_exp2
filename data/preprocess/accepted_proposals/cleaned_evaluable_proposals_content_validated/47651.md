==== [Issue Body] ====
Per chat elsewhere, reflect.Ptr is weird and doesn't match reflect.UnsafePointer or unsafe.Pointer.

Proposal: add reflect.Pointer matching reflect.Ptr.

Likewise reflect.PtrTo -> reflect.PointerTo probably.
